>> Overview

The DigiLute began with a loosely defined goal- to make a musical instrument that incorporates the performer's body in its playing. I wanted to create something that was equally visual and audible. I knew I was looking to make something fun, as the project's main inspirations are rooted in fantasy and video games. I spent time creating prototypes using large, taped-together cardboard pieces.
